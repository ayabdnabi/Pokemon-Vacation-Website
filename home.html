WELCOME TO ALOLA 

Join Ash Ketchum and his companion Pikachu on their summer vacation to Hawaii (known as Alola)! On his journey, he meets new Pokémon in order to become the best Pokémon Master. Along the way, he gets familar with the region and the culture during his stay. He also makes new friends and comes across different islands which all have different trial captains in which he must defeat.

What more might happen during his stay in Alola? Will he be able to defeat all the captains? What types of Pokémon will he meet? Let's find out what exciting things might happen this summer!
