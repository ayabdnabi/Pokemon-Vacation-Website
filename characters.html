Meet the main protagonist in the series, Ash Ketchum! He is portrayed as energetic, determined, and optimistic. He's always eager to learn new things and improve his skills as a Pokémon trainer. He's also very competitive and enjoys a good challenge, often pushing himself to the limit in battles.

Ash is shown to be caring and supportive of his friends, always willing to lend a helping hand or provide encouragement when they need it. He's also known for his strong sense of justice, often standing up for what's right and defending those who can't defend themselves.

Despite his positive qualities, Ash can also be stubborn and impulsive at times, which can get him into trouble. He's also known for making mistakes and learning from them, which is a key part of his character development throughout the series.
Next is the mysterious assisstant, Lillie! She is a rather shy and timid character. She is hesitant to interact with people and Pokemon and is often uncomfortable in social situations. However, as the series progresses, Lillie begins to develop her confidence and assertiveness, thanks to her experiences with her friends and her involvement in the island challenge.

Lillie is also shown to be intelligent and resourceful, often providing important insights and ideas that help Ash and the others overcome obstacles. She has a strong sense of empathy and cares deeply for her friends and Pokemon.

Lillie's character is also shaped by her complex relationship with her mother, who is a powerful figure in the Alola region. Her mother's expectations and pressure on Lillie are a source of tension and conflict throughout the series.
Meet the carefree and loving Professor Kukui who is passionate about research and discovery. He is the region's Pokemon professor and also serves as the host of the island challenge, making him a respected figure in the Alola region.

Kukui is known for his energetic and casual personality. He enjoys battling and is a skilled trainer himself, often participating in battles alongside Ash and the others.

Despite his laid-back demeanor, Kukui is also a dedicated researcher who is constantly exploring new areas of the region and uncovering new information about Pokemon. He cares deeply for his students and is always willing to lend a helping hand, whether it's in the form of advice or a friendly Pokemon battle.
