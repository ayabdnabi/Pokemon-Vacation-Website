PLOT 

The Pokémon Sun and Moon series follows the adventures of Ash Ketchum as he spends his time in the Alola region, where he meets new friends and participates in the island challenge.  

Ash arrives in Alola with his friend Pikachu and is immediately drawn into the unique culture and customs of the region. He meets Professor Kukui, who introduces him to the island challenge, a series of trials and battles that he must complete in order to become a true Pokémon trainer. 

 Along the way, Ash makes new friends, including Lillie, a shy girl who is the assistant to Professor Kukui, and the energetic and confident trainers Kiawe, Mallow, Sophocles, and Lana. Together, they explore the region and discover new Pokémon, including the legendary guardian deities of each island.
Ash faces off against various Trial Captains, each of whom presents him with a unique challenge that tests his skills as a trainer. He also encounters the villainous Team Skull, a group of mischievous and trouble-making trainers who cause chaos throughout the region.

As Ash and his friends' journey through Alola, they encounter mysterious Ultra Beasts that threaten the safety of the region. Ash and his friends must work together to stop them from causing destruction.

 Ash participates in the Alola League, a tournament to determine the strongest trainer in the region. He emerges victorious at the end of the battles, completing his wild but fun vacation in Alola!
